I started coding at **16** — not to build apps, but to **understand how systems break**. I came from the cybersecurity underground, learned by exploring what others couldn't see, and eventually turned that mindset into building products.

I skipped university. I skipped the "right path." I learned by doing things nobody teaches in a classroom. Now I channel all of that into **building startups and AI products**.

When AI came along, I became the **kid who found an infinite box of toys**. I'm not just using AI — I'm building with it, on it, around it. Every single day.
